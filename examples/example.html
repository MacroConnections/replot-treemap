Replot Treemap: Examples

Basic Example

In this example, we rely completely on Replot's smart defaults and simply plug in the data and allow Replot to do the rest. We get a beautiful interactive treemap with nested layers with just two lines of code.

Advanced Example

In this example, we explore Replot's customizability, adding a tooltip, manipulating the colors through a color function and building a control panel where the user can manipulate chart features.
